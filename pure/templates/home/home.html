Admin

A college admin is a person who is responsible for managing and maintaining their college's presence on a platform. They have the ability to send announcements, manage courses, approve faculty accounts, and assign custom roles to students within their college.

Faculty

Faculty members are individuals who are responsible for teaching courses at a college. They have access to tools and resources that are designed to help them connect with their students and manage their courses, including the ability to make announcements and participate in a faculty-only chat.

Student

Students are the primary users of the platform. They can access various features and capabilities including chat rooms, announcements etc. Students have access to college-wide and class-specific chat rooms. The platform is designed to provide students with tools and resources that help them connect with their peers, teachers, and the college community and aims to support and enhance the student experience by helping them stay connected and engaged.
